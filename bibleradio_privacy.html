Privacy Policy for Bible Radio for Seniors

Last Updated: June 2026

Thank you for using Bible Radio for Seniors ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Bible Radio for Seniors is designed to provide access to publicly available Christian radio streams. We do not require account registration.

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Phone number
  • Government identification
  • Payment information

Device Information

The App may automatically collect limited technical information including device type, operating system version, language settings, and anonymous diagnostic information. This information is used solely to improve app performance and stability.

Radio Content

Bible Radio for Seniors uses publicly available radio station information provided through the Radio Browser project and other publicly accessible streaming sources.

The App acts solely as a directory and player for publicly available radio streams. We do not host, own, store, transmit, modify, or control the radio content provided by third-party stations.

All trademarks, logos, audio content, broadcasts, and intellectual property rights remain the property of their respective owners.

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services that may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies, including:

  • Apple App Store
  • Apple Analytics, if enabled
  • Advertising providers, if applicable
  • Radio Browser API

Copyright Notice

Bible Radio for Seniors does not claim ownership of any third-party radio station content. All broadcasts are provided by their respective stations through publicly available streaming sources.

If you are a copyright owner and believe that content available through the App infringes your rights, please contact us. Upon receiving a valid notice, we will promptly review the request and remove the relevant station or content when appropriate.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information processed by the App. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App and reflected by the updated date above.
