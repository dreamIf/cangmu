Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile applications, websites, and related services collect, use, store, and protect information. This policy is designed to apply generally to apps published by us unless a specific app provides a separate privacy policy.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us at cangmut@outlook.com.

Information We Collect

We aim to collect only the information needed to provide and improve our apps. Depending on the features you use, we may collect or process the following types of information:

  • Information you choose to provide, such as feedback, support requests, or email messages.
  • Device and app information, such as device type, operating system version, app version, language, region, and basic diagnostic data.
  • Approximate or precise location information, only when an app feature requires it and you grant permission.
  • Notification preferences, reminder settings, display preferences, or other app settings stored on your device.
  • Purchase or subscription status when the app offers paid features, free trials, or in-app purchases.

How We Use Information

We may use information for the following purposes:

  • To provide core app features and personalize the app experience.
  • To calculate location-based results when a location-based feature is enabled.
  • To send local reminders or notifications when you request them.
  • To process, verify, and restore purchases or subscriptions.
  • To respond to support requests, bug reports, and other communications.
  • To maintain app security, fix issues, improve performance, and develop new features.

Location Information

Some apps may request access to your location to provide features such as local times, directions, nearby results, or other location-based calculations. Location access is used only for the feature you choose to use. You can enable, disable, or change location permissions at any time in your device settings.

Notifications

Some apps may ask permission to send notifications, such as reminders, alerts, or status updates. You can manage notification permissions at any time in your device settings.

In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

If an app offers in-app purchases or subscriptions, payment processing is handled by the applicable app store, such as Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not receive or store your full payment card details. We may receive information needed to verify purchase status, unlock paid features, provide customer support, and prevent fraud.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use third-party services for functions such as app distribution, analytics, crash reporting, purchase management, cloud services, or customer support. These services may process information according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services used by the specific app.

Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Information stored locally on your device may remain until you delete it, reset the app, or uninstall the app.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under the age required by applicable law. If you believe that a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.
