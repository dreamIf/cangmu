MutePhoto Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 16, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to MutePhoto (referred to as “the App,” “we,” or “MutePhoto”). We value your privacy and data security. This document explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect the personal and non-personal information you provide when using MutePhoto. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using the App.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide

  • Account and Contact Information: such as your email address if you create an account or contact us (e.g., for support or billing receipts).
  • Content Data: photos, videos, and related metadata (such as timestamps or location tags, unless removed by you upon upload) that you import, upload, or store in the App.
  • Payment and Subscription Information: purchase records (via the Apple App Store) and necessary data to verify subscription status. We do not collect credit card numbers directly; all payments are processed through Apple’s system.

2. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device and Usage Data: device type, operating system version, device identifiers (anonymous or hashed), app version, crash logs, usage behavior (e.g., feature usage frequency), and performance data.
  • Analytics and Performance Data: aggregated or anonymized data to help us improve product performance and user experience.

3. Information from Third Parties

If you use MutePhoto through third-party services (such as social login, cloud storage, or other integrated services), we may receive necessary information from these providers. We only process such data where required or with your consent.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the App’s core functions (e.g., encrypted storage, album access, photo management).
  • To process payments, subscriptions, and verify subscription status (via Apple’s App Store system).
  • To improve the App, fix errors, analyze usage, and enhance performance.
  • To send you service-related notifications, billing information, or support responses (and, if you consent, promotional communications).
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud, misuse, or security incidents.

Data Storage, Encryption, and Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or loss. Sensitive data (such as private photos) stored on your device or transmitted through the App may be encrypted. We also recommend enabling device-level encryption and lock screen security provided by your operating system.

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We regularly review and update our security practices.

Third-Party Service Providers

We may engage third-party providers (e.g., analytics, cloud backup, crash logging, customer support, in-app purchase validation, or hosting services) to support the App’s functionality. These providers may process or access certain data on our behalf. We review third parties to ensure they comply with applicable data protection obligations.

Examples may include analytics platforms, crash monitoring tools, email providers, and cloud storage services. For a current list of service providers, please contact us using the details below.

Payments, Subscriptions, and Auto-Renewals

  • All payments and subscriptions are handled through the Apple App Store. Apple processes your payment information; we do not directly collect or store credit card details.
  • Subscriptions automatically renew according to the period you select, unless you cancel through your Apple ID account settings.
  • We may use App Store receipts to verify subscription status and enable features accordingly.

Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. If you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymize most personal data within a reasonable timeframe, though backups may persist longer.

Your Rights (Where Applicable)

If you are located in the EU/EEA, UK, or other jurisdictions with applicable laws, you may have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: request deletion of your personal data.
  • Restriction & Objection Rights: restrict or object to how we process your data in certain cases.
  • Data Portability: request your data in a portable format, where technically feasible.

To exercise these rights, please contact us via the details in the “Contact Us” section below. We will respond within the time required by applicable laws.

Additional Disclosures for California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you may have rights to access, delete, and opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. MutePhoto does not sell personal information. Please contact us if you have questions or wish to exercise your rights.

Children’s Privacy

MutePhoto is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data without parental consent, we will promptly delete it. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country or region, to provide and maintain the App. We take appropriate legal and technical measures to safeguard data during cross-border transfers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If there are significant changes, we will notify you via the App, email, or by posting a notice on this page. Each update will include an “Effective Date.” We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
